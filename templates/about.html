Why I made this website

I have always loved being in nature. I find working on farms fulfilling and deeply cherish my connection with the earth. To me, there are not many pleasures higher than digging my hand into dirt and taking care of vegitables and produce. It feels to me like a physical meditation and paying homage to humanity.

I was raised bi-culturally in China and in the Bay Area. Working on farms in Montana right after college gave me a glimpse into what living and working in a non-coastal metropolis feels like. I have made friends with humans and animals alike. I am about to visit the farms I worked on, which was the inspiration for making this website

Many friends of mine have asked how I found farms. Here I hope that you are able to play with the idea of working on farms and staying with host families by using my website.
